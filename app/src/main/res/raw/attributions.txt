Although the code is mine, the other resources I used to make this app are not. Below is a Works Cited
for my text source. Language of Flowers is a work belonging to the public domain and made available
by Project Gutenberg as a plain text file in the UTF-8 encoding, which I used with some modifications.

Greenaway, Kate. Language of Flowers. Project Gutenberg, www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/31591/pg31591.txt. Accessed 10 June 2020.

All images I used are free to reuse under a compatible license. I did not modify any of these images; I am simply
using them to show what a given flower looks like. I will source them to the best of my ability below
(some credit is given to a username in place of a legal name).
